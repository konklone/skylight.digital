Solving important problems

Every day millions of people — from veterans to immigrants — depend on government services to fulfill their needs. But too often these services are delivered using outmoded policies, practices, and technologies, resulting in frustrating user experiences and inefficient use of taxpayer dollars.

Enter Skylight, a HUBZone-certified digital consultancy based out of Chapel Hill, North Carolina. We’re the founders behind successful startups inside and outside of government, including 18F and TalkTo.

Our mission is to make government work in a digital world using design, technology, and procurement.

About us

Services designed for your success

To succeed in today’s public-service environment, you need to think and be digital. But getting there often requires fundamental practice, culture, and technology change. And that means institutionalizing modern disciplines such as human-centered design, lean-agile engineering, data science, and modular contracting.

We offer services in a number of areas to accelerate your journey to becoming a digital-first agency: Digital Services Delivery, Digital Procurement, and Digital Transformation.

These services are designed not only to deliver results, but to also transfer our knowledge of modern principles and practices to you. To facilitate this learning process, we employ a unique practice called embedded instructional design. Even further, our guilds (communities of practice) make all of our delivery assets (for example, methodologies) completely free, open, and accessible to you.

How we work with you is as important as what we work on together.
